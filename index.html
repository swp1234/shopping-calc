About Shopping Calculator

DopaBrain Shopping Calculator combines real-time currency conversion, customs duty estimation, and country-specific tipping guide in one tool. Know your true cost when shopping internationally or traveling. Completely free.

How to Use the Shopping Calculator

Choose from Exchange Rate, Customs Duty, or Tip Calculator tabs. Convert currencies with real-time rates, estimate customs costs for international purchases, or calculate tips based on country-specific tipping customs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are exchange rates real-time?

Yes, we use real-time exchange rate APIs. If the API is unavailable, fallback fixed rates are used with a warning displayed.

How are customs duties calculated?

Based on product price, shipping cost, purchase country, and product category. Items under $150 are duty-free.

Which currencies are supported?

Major currencies including USD, EUR, JPY, CNY, GBP, AUD, CAD, THB, VND, SGD, and KRW.

Which countries does the tip calculator support?

Major travel destinations including the USA, Canada, UK, France, Japan, and China.

Is this calculator free?

Yes, all features are completely free. No registration or payment required.
